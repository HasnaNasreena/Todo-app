About Us

Established in 2015 in Taif, Saudi Arabia, Almanjal has grown to become a trusted leader in the distribution and marketing of premium feed additives and veterinary products. With a strong commitment to quality and innovation, we partner with global manufacturers to bring world-class solutions to the local market. Our products meet the highest standards of safety, effectiveness, and reliability, ensuring better health, growth, and well-being of both livestock and companion animals.

Our Mission

At Almanjal, our mission is to provide premium-quality feed additives and veterinary products that ensure the health and well-being of animals. We carefully source from trusted international manufacturers, delivering only the most effective and safe products. By combining innovative solutions with technical expertise, we aim to support farmers, veterinarians, and animal owners in achieving higher productivity and farm efficiency while promoting better animal care.

Services

We specialize in the distribution of high-quality feed additives and veterinary medicines, ensuring that animals receive the best care. Through global sourcing and efficient logistics, we provide timely and reliable product delivery. With strong technical expertise, our team also offers guidance on product usage, helping farmers and veterinarians maximize efficiency. We deliver market-driven solutions to meet the evolving needs of the animal health industry.
